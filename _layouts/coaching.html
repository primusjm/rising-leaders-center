We use Integral Coaching to help leaders and organizations grow from the inside out by deepening their self-awareness, aligning with their values, developing new skills, instilling balance across all areas of their lives, and building their capacity to lead transformational change.

Integral Coaching is different from other types of coaching because it honors the individual to create and instill balance within individuals, their teams, and their communities. Coaching is not a quick fix to our challenges as leaders, it’s a powerful, long-term investment in ourselves, our leadership, and our community. In coaching, a person works with a trained, certified coach who helps them to tackle personal and professional challenges in order to rise to become their best self.

By looking through a multitude of lenses and working to better understand ourselves, our eyes and hearts open to new ways of looking, feeling, connecting, and leading.

For instance, a CRL coach might guide you through:

  • Understanding systems and their dynamics.
  • Clarifying your leadership identity, tendencies, and values.
  • Prioritizing wellness and sustainability.
  • Strengthening organizational culture and impact strategies.

We have experience working with clients from a wide variety of countries and cultural contexts including the UK, Brazil, Canada, East Africa, and South Africa.
